    About the Band

    Frankie Stein and Drake Yula, roommates at the University of Monsters, were an electrifying duo: Frankie’s vocals crackled with energy, while Drake’s hypnotic drumming commanded every stage. Meanwhile, at rival school Fear Institute of Technology, brothers Wulf Mann and Invis Ible Mann were making their own mark—Wulf’s thunderous Bass paired with Invis’s hauntingly beautiful Guitar melodies created an otherworldly sound. Fate brought the four together at a prestigious monster music competition, where Frankie and Drake claimed victory in the finals.

    But rather than let rivalry linger, they recognized the unique talent of Wulf and Invis and decided to unite their powers, forming Stage Fright, a band destined to rock the monster world like never before.

    Frankie Stein

    Vocal

    Frankie Stein, the electric soul of Stage Fright, commands the stage with his fiery guitar riffs that electrify the audience. A prodigy from the University of Monsters, his mastery over the guitar turns every performance into a charged spectacle, leaving his fans spellbound and begging for more.

    Drake Yula

    Drums

    Drake Yula, the hypnotic voice of Stage Fright, mesmerizes audiences with his powerful and enchanting vocals. Known for his commanding stage presence and ability to captivate with every note, Drake’s performances are both alluring and intense, cementing his place as the charismatic frontman of the band.

    Wulf Mann

    Bass

    Wulf Mann, the thunderous backbone of Stage Fright, delivers powerful and resonant bass lines that shake the very foundation of their music. His explosive playing style and raw energy, honed at the Fear Institute of Technology, make him an indispensable force, driving the rhythm with unwavering intensity.

    Invis Ible Mann

    Guitar

    Invis Ible Mann, the spectral guitarist of Stage Fright, weaves hauntingly beautiful melodies that add an otherworldly dimension to the band’s sound. His ethereal presence and innovative guitar work create a mesmerizing aura, transforming every song into a captivating sonic journey that lingers long after the final note.
